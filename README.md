🚀 Domain Forge AI: ML-Powered Prioritization Engine for Strategic Website Redesign
📌 Overview

Domain Forge AI is an intelligent Machine Learning-powered platform that helps organizations identify and prioritize website domains or subdomains that require redesign. Instead of relying solely on traditional analytics, the system combines user behavior patterns, business metrics, predictive analytics, and anomaly detection to generate actionable recommendations for strategic website improvements.

By analyzing traffic, conversion rates, customer lifetime value, churn risk, and operational issues, Domain Forge AI calculates a comprehensive Domain Impact Score and produces a prioritized redesign roadmap for decision-makers.

🎯 Problem Statement

Organizations managing multiple websites, domains, or subdomains often face challenges such as:

Difficulty identifying which domains need redesign first.
Large volumes of analytics data without actionable insights.
Resource wastage due to poorly prioritized redesign efforts.
Lack of predictive analysis regarding business impact.

Current analytics platforms provide descriptive metrics but do not recommend where redesign investments should be focused.

💡 Solution

Domain Forge AI transforms raw website analytics into strategic recommendations through:

User Journey Analysis
Behavioral Segmentation
Predictive Modeling
Domain Impact Scoring
Redesign Prioritization
What-if Scenario Simulation

The platform empowers stakeholders to make data-driven redesign decisions that maximize business outcomes.
